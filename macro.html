Macro Calculator

How to Use This Macro Calculator

Enter your daily calorie intake. The calculator will automatically split your calories into protein, carbs, and fats based on a balanced ratio.

Example

If you enter 2,000 calories:

Protein: 150g
Carbs: 200g
Fat: 67g

Frequently Asked Questions

What are macros?
Macros are protein, carbohydrates, and fats that make up your daily diet.

Is this ratio customizable?
This version uses a balanced 30/40/30 split.

Can I use this for weight loss?
Yes, by adjusting total calories.
