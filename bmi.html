Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMI?
BMI (Body Mass Index) is a number calculated from your height and weight that estimates body fat level.

Is BMI accurate?
It is a general guideline. It does not account for muscle mass, so athletes may have higher BMI but low body fat.

What is a healthy BMI range?
18.5 to 24.9 is considered a normal healthy range.

Does this calculator use US units?
Yes — this calculator uses pounds, feet, and inches.

Can BMI determine health?
It is a screening tool, not a full health diagnosis.
