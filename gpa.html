How to Use This Calculator

Enter your letter grades (A, B, C, D, or F) into the boxes above. You can leave extra boxes blank. Click “Calculate GPA” to get your result instantly.

Example

If you enter: A, B, B, C
The calculator converts them into points:
A = 4.0, B = 3.0, C = 2.0

Result: GPA = 3.00

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need credits?
No, this is a simple GPA calculator.

Is this accurate?
Yes for standard unweighted GPA systems.

Can I use plus/minus grades?
Not in this version (A+, B- not included).
