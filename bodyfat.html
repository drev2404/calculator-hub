Body Fat Calculator

How This Body Fat Calculator Works

This calculator estimates your body fat percentage using height, weight, age, and gender. It gives you a general idea of your fitness level and body composition.

Example

A 180 lb male who is 5'10" and 30 years old will typically fall between 15–20% body fat depending on activity level and muscle mass.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this medically exact?
No, it is an estimate based on formulas.

Does muscle affect results?
Yes, muscular people may show higher readings.

Is it useful for fitness tracking?
Yes, for general progress tracking.
