Body Fat Calculator

How It Works

Uses BMI-based estimation with US units (lbs + feet/inches).

Example

200 lbs, 5'11", age 30 → ~18–22% body fat range

Frequently Asked Questions

What is body fat percentage?
It estimates how much of your body is fat compared to lean mass (muscle, water, bone).

Is this body fat calculator accurate?
It provides a good estimate but is not as precise as medical methods like DEXA scans.

What is a healthy body fat range?
Men: 10–20%
Women: 18–28%

Does this use US units?
Yes — pounds, feet, and inches are used.

Why is it based on BMI?
This calculator uses a BMI-based formula to estimate body fat when no equipment is used.
