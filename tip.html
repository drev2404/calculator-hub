Tip Calculator

How to Use the Tip Calculator

Enter your bill amount and tip percentage to quickly calculate the tip and total bill. This is perfect for dining out or splitting bills with friends.

Example

A $50 restaurant bill with a 20% tip results in a $10 tip, making the total $60.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a normal tip?
Usually 15–20% in the US.

Do I tip before tax?
It depends, but usually after tax.
