Loan Payoff Calculator

How This Loan Payoff Calculator Works

This calculator estimates how long it will take to fully pay off your loan based on your balance, monthly payment, and interest rate. It helps you plan debt payoff strategies.

Example

A $10,000 loan with a $300 monthly payment may take about 36 months to pay off, depending on interest rate and extra payments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can extra payments help?
Yes, they reduce payoff time and interest.

Does interest stay fixed?
It depends on loan type.

Is this accurate?
It is an estimate, not a bank statement.
