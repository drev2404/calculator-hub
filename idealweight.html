Frequently Asked Questions

What is ideal body weight?
It is an estimate of a healthy weight range based on height and gender.

Is this medically exact?
No, it is a general guideline used for fitness and health planning.

Why are there male and female values?
Body composition differences affect average healthy weight ranges.

Can athletes use this?
Yes, but athletes may naturally weigh more due to muscle mass.
