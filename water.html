Water Intake Calculator

How to Use This Calculator

Enter your body weight in pounds. The calculator estimates how many ounces of water you should drink per day.

Example

If you weigh 180 lbs:

180 × 0.67 = 121 oz of water per day

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is water intake based on weight?
Larger bodies require more hydration for proper function.

Does exercise change this?
Yes, active people should drink more.

Is this exact?
It is an estimate, not a strict medical rule.
