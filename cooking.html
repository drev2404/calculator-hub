How It Works

This calculator converts common cooking measurements used in recipes. It includes both US and metric kitchen units.

Example

1 cup = 16 tablespoons = 48 teaspoons
1 liter ≈ 4.23 cups
1 gallon = 3.78 liters

FAQ

Is this exact for cooking?
Yes for standard kitchen conversions.

Does this work for dry and liquid ingredients?
Liquid conversions are exact. Dry (grams) depends on ingredient density.
