Mortgage Calculator

How This Mortgage Calculator Works

Enter your home price, interest rate, and loan term to estimate your monthly mortgage payment. This helps you understand affordability and long-term costs.

Example

A $300,000 mortgage at 6% over 30 years results in lower monthly payments than a 15-year loan, but significantly more total interest paid.

Frequently Asked Questions

What affects mortgage payments?
Loan amount, interest rate, and loan term.

Is a shorter loan better?
Yes, it reduces total interest paid.
