Salary Calculator

How the Salary Calculator Works

Enter your hourly wage and weekly hours to estimate your annual salary. This tool helps compare job offers and understand your yearly income.

Example

If you earn $25/hour and work 40 hours per week, your annual salary is about $52,000 before taxes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this include taxes?
No, it calculates gross income only.

What if I work overtime?
You can adjust hours per week manually.

Is this accurate?
Yes for base salary estimates.
