Retirement Calculator

How This Retirement Calculator Works

This calculator estimates how much money you will have at retirement based on your current savings, monthly contributions, and expected annual return. It helps you plan long-term financial goals.

Example

If you start with $20,000, contribute $500/month, and earn 7% annual return, your retirement savings can grow significantly over 30 years due to compound interest.

Frequently Asked Questions

What return rate should I use?
5–8% is commonly used for long-term estimates.

Does inflation matter?
Yes, real value may be lower over time.

Is this exact?
No, it’s an estimate based on assumptions.
