How This Time Zone Converter Works

This tool automatically detects your current time zone and converts the time you enter into another location. It uses real-world time zone data, including daylight savings adjustments.

Example

If it is 3:00 PM in your location, converting to London will automatically adjust for daylight savings and show the correct local time there.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this handle daylight savings time?
Yes, it automatically adjusts based on the selected location and current date.

How does it detect my location?
It uses your browser’s time zone settings, not GPS.

Is this accurate worldwide?
Yes, it uses built-in global time zone data.
