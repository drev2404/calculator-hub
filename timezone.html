Time Zone Converter

How This Time Zone Converter Works

This calculator converts time between different global time zones by applying the hour difference. Simply enter a time, choose your current time zone, and select a destination time zone.

Example

If it is 3:00 PM in New York (EST), converting to London (UTC) will show 8:00 PM.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this include daylight savings time?
No, this version uses fixed time offsets.

Can I convert any time zone?
Yes, select from the available options.

Is this real-time?
No, it converts based on the time you enter.
